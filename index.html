Jon Peirce, PhD

Scientist, programmer, dad

Programming Research Publications Bio

Summary:

Jon is a Professor at School of Psychology, University of Nottingham, where he is Chair of Psychology Research Methods.

He has spent over 20 years studying the neural mechanisms of visual perception, trying improve research methods and tools, and teaching, predominantly on research methods.

Jon is best known for developing PsychoPy, free software for behavioural science experiments.

He founded Open Science Tools Ltd in 2019 to support the continued development of PsychoPy and Pavlovia.
